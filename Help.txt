Instructions:
Melody is open source music software where you can listen songs.
It also comes with the feature where you can create and save you own playlist.

Controls:
Ctrl-N : To create a new playlist.
Ctrl-S : To save playlist.
Ctrl-Shift-S : to save the playlist with another name.
Ctrl-O : To load your saved playlists.
'+' : to increase volume
'-' : to decrease volume
Esc : to come back from the albums folder/open playlist window.
spacebar : to pause and play song
'M' : to mute/unmute songs
